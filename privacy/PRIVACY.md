# StrideGuide Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** January 2025  
**Version:** 2.0

## Overview

StrideGuide is an accessibility assistant designed with privacy by design. Your camera stays on your device, your data remains under your control, and we collect only what's necessary to provide you with safe navigation assistance.

## What We Collect

### Camera Data
- **Your camera never leaves your device**
- All video processing happens locally on your device
- No images or video are uploaded to our servers
- Camera data is processed in real-time and immediately discarded

### Emergency Information
- Emergency contact details (name, phone, relationship) - stored locally on your device
- Location data (only when SOS is activated and only for emergency services)
- SMS messages sent only during actual emergencies

### Optional Cloud Features
- If you enable cloud descriptions, anonymous scene descriptions may be processed
- No personally identifiable information is included in cloud requests
- You can disable cloud features at any time in Settings

### Usage Analytics (Optional)
- Basic app usage patterns (features used, session duration)
- Device type and performance metrics (to improve accessibility)
- **No personal information, location data, or camera content**
- You can opt out completely in Privacy Settings

## How We Use Your Information

### Essential Functions
- **Guidance:** Camera data processed locally for obstacle detection
- **Lost Item Finder:** Item patterns stored encrypted on your device
- **Emergency SOS:** Contact information used only for emergency notifications

### Optional Improvements
- Anonymous usage patterns help us improve accessibility features
- Performance data helps optimize the app for different devices
- Feedback helps us prioritize new features

## Your Data Rights

### Complete Control
- **View:** See all data stored about you in Settings > Privacy
- **Export:** Download your data in standard formats
- **Delete:** Remove all data with one tap
- **Disable:** Turn off any data collection

### Emergency Contacts
- Add, edit, or remove emergency contacts anytime
- Contacts are stored encrypted on your device only
- SMS notifications sent only during genuine emergencies

### Machine Learning Data
- Item patterns you teach are stored encrypted locally
- Delete individual learned items or all data anytime
- No learned patterns are shared with us or third parties

## Data Storage & Security

### Local Encryption
- All sensitive data encrypted using device-bound keys
- Emergency contacts protected by device encryption
- Learned item patterns stored in encrypted format

### No Cloud Storage
- Your personal data stays on your device
- No user accounts or profiles on our servers
- No synchronization across devices

### Secure Deletion
- "Delete All Data" completely removes all information
- Deleted data cannot be recovered
- Secure deletion follows industry best practices

## Emergency Features

### SOS Functionality
- Long-press triggers emergency SMS to your contacts
- Message includes your location and timestamp
- 3-second cancellation window with voice activation
- No data stored after emergency resolves

### Location Services
- Used only during active SOS situations
- Shared only with your designated emergency contacts
- Location sharing stops when emergency ends
- No location history maintained

## Children's Privacy

StrideGuide is designed for users 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided information to us, please contact us immediately.

## Third-Party Services

### Minimal Dependencies
- No social media integrations
- No advertising networks
- No analytics trackers
- Optional cloud AI with strict privacy controls

### Emergency Services
- SMS messages sent through your device's messaging system
- No third-party messaging services used
- Your mobile carrier's privacy policy applies to SMS

## Legal Compliance

### Canada (PIPEDA)
- Consent required for all data collection
- Purpose limitation: data used only as described
- Right to access, correct, and delete your information
- Breach notification within legal timeframes

### International Users
- Data processing follows your local privacy laws
- Additional protections for EU users under GDPR
- Right to data portability in standard formats

## Updates to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will:
- Notify you of significant changes through the app
- Maintain previous versions for your reference
- Never reduce your privacy protections without clear consent
